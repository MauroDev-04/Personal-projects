Movie Review: The bad guys 2

The reformed band of try-hard animals must spring back into action when a group of cunning female criminals, "The Bad Girls," forces them to join them for a global heist that will test their loyalty and skills as they try to not only prove their newfound goodness, but also confront a dangerous plot by the villains to steal all the gold on the planet.
